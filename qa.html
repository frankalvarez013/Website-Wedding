What about COVID?
------

For your safety and the safety of others, the venue requires ALL attendants to have proof of being COVID negative three days before the event. Please bring your CDC vaccination card with you or a negative covid test since they will be checking at the entrance.

What Should I do if I can't Make it?
------

You will be dearly missed! If you can not make it to the wedding, please let us know as soon as possible and RSVP "no", so that we can plan accordingly. Thank you!

Can I Bring Additional Guests Not Noted on my Invitation?
------

We have addressed the invitations to the people we would love to come. If you received a plus one, they will appear under your name when you RSVP. Please ensure that you are RSVPing for yourself & your guest. Due to limited space, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your wedding invitation.

Is There a Dress Code?
------

We would love to see our family and friends get dressed up with us! We are requesting a formal dress code for our evening. Wear whatever color you like, but please, do not wear white.

Are Children Invited?
------

We love all of your little ones! However, due to many factors including limited space, we respectfully request that you only bring children that are included on your invitation.

Is There Parking Nearby?
------

Yes! The venue has a very large parking lot capable of fitting all guests joining us.

Will Alcoholic Beverages be Provided?
------

Yes! There will a cash bar where you could purchase your own alcoholic drinks. The venue has made it clear to not bring any alcoholic drinks or bottles with you, if caught, the venue will confiscate them.
